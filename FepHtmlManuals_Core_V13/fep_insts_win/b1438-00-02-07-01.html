2.7.1 TCP/IP Protocol

FUJITSU Enterprise Postgres supports version 4 and 6 (IPv4 and IPv6) of TCP/IP protocols.

Note

Do not use link-local addresses if TCP/IP protocol version 6 addresses are used.
